Upcoming Events

One of the ways we support students is holding different types of events. These events are always free and open to all LaunchDeck members. Some types of events we host include speaker panels, fireside chats, networking round tables, workshops to learn new skills, and hackathons. Check out some events we have coming up!

Weekly LaunchTalks

LaunchTalks features a distinguished guest in tech and entrepreneurship every week. Hear the best insights from the best in their field.

Meiri Anto '17 | Thursday, 2/25 @ 8 PM EST | Zoom

Meiri Anto ‘17 is a senior software engineer at Ladder and a member of the South Park Commons, and her passion lies in the intersection of technology and social impact. In her past experiences, she’s interned and worked as a SWE at numerous tech companies including Coursera, Yelp, Facebook, and Apple. She’s worked at tech firms of all sizes, from seed-stage startups to large companies with thousands of employees. She’s particularly interested in mission-driven tech, and is currently pursuing a path that promotes upward mobility for America. Come join us to speak with her in our second LaunchTalk event!

Shannon Hsu | Saturday, 3/6 @ 8 PM EST | Zoom

Shannon Hsu is a Product Manager at Lyft and received her Bachelors of Arts in Mathematics at Williams College in 2015. She went on to complete her MBA at Harvard in 2020 where she familiarized herself with the management and scaling of tech ventures. In her past experiences, she’s taken on various roles at tech, finance, and consulting firms including Mckinsey & Company, realtor.com, and Barclays. She’s very passionate about the work that she does and has offered to share her knowledge and experiences with members of the LaunchDeck community! Come join us at our third LaunchTalk to hear and speak to her over Zoom!

Jeremy Schifeling '03 | Thursday, 3/11 @ 8 PM EST | Zoom

Jeremy Schifeling ‘03 is the Principal Product Marketing Manager at Khan Academy and is a tech marketing leader with a 15-year track record of driving results. In his past experiences, he’s served as the VP of Marketing for two enterprise SaaS startups, led product marketing for LinkedIn's student products, and went from interning on Apple's iOS product marketing team to hiring and mentoring world-class marketers across Silicon Valley. He’s super excited to share his knowledge and expertise with members of the LaunchDeck community; so come join us at our fourth LaunchTalk to hear and speak to him over Zoom!
