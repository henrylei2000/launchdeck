Our Programs

We hope to be able to provide students with the necessary skills and resources to succeed in their careers and entrepreneurial endeavors through various experiential opportunities and funding initiatives. Here are some of the programs we offer to all LaunchDeck members.

Launch Fellows

Launch Fellows are distinguished underclassmen LaunchDeck members selected to participate in a semester-long mentorship program with an upperclassmen with internship experience in their field of interests.

Learn More

Project Funding

Have a startup idea you want to try to build out or is there a new skill or technology you want to learn? Apply to get funding from LaunchDeck.

Learn More

Weekly LaunchTalks

Each week, LaunchDeck hosts a casual fireside chat with a successful alum in tech or entrepreneurship. Come learn, network, and ask any burning questions you have!

Learn More

Launch School

Launch School feaures a series of workshops from 2-time YCombinator Finalist Andy Lee on how to take your startup from 0 to 1.
