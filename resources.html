Our Resources

At LaunchDeck, one of our goals is to enable students to succeed in their career and entrepreneurial ventures by hosting events/workshops and sharing helpful resources or opportunities.

Here’s a repository of recordings and slidedecks past LaunchDeck events, mobile development workshops, and a curated guide of helpful resources LaunchDeck team members have used to secure internships at companies like Salesforce and Amazon. Make sure to also check out our blog for more great resources.

Past Speaker Events

Swarthmore Alumni in Tech - Explore Different Careers Panel

Learn from panelists from companies like Uber, Google, Yahoo, YCombinator across roles like software engineering, product management, and data science about best practices on how to break into the industry, big company vs. small company, how Swarthmore helped their career trajectory and more.

From Good to Great: Lessons from the Tech Industry from Cathy Polinsky ‘99

A casual chat with Cathy Polinsky, former CTO @ Stitch Fix and former SVP of Engineering at Salesforce on her own take of From Good to Great. Hear her discuss what differentiates a great company, a great engineer, a great leader or a great mentor from just a good one as she shares life lessons and stories from her career journey in the tech industry.

LaunchTalk 1 with Danielle Strachman | 02.18.21 | Youtube

Danielle Strachman
Danielle Strachman is a co-founder and general partner of 1517 Fund and a senior advisor to the Thiel Fellowship where she supports entrepreneurs with grant, pre-seed, and seed funding for technology startups. Her main goal through her work is to bring out the hidden talent of young entrepreneurs and innovators, namely those who aren’t geared towards the path of higher education. With her expertise in the sectors of technology, entrepreneurship, and education, she guides and prepares the entrepreneurs of tomorrow. Come join us to speak with her in our first ever LaunchTalk event!

LaunchTalk 2 with Meiri Anto | 02.25.21 | Youtube

Meiri Anto
Meiri Anto ‘17 is a senior software engineer at Dreambound and a member of the South Park Commons, and her passion lies in the intersection of technology and social impact. In her past experiences, she’s interned and worked as a SWE at numerous tech companies including Coursera, Yelp, Facebook, and Apple. She’s worked at tech firms of all sizes, from seed-stage startups to large companies with thousands of employees. She’s particularly interested in mission-driven tech, and is currently pursuing a path that promotes upward mobility for America. Come join us to speak with her in our second LaunchTalk event!
