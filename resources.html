Our Resources

At LaunchDeck, one of our goals is to enable students to succeed in their career and entrepreneurial ventures by hosting events/workshops and sharing helpful resources or opportunities.

Here’s a repository of recordings and slidedecks past LaunchDeck events, mobile development workshops, and a curated guide of helpful resources LaunchDeck team members have used to secure internships at companies like Salesforce and Amazon:

Past Speaker Events

Swarthmore Alumni in Tech - Explore Different Careers Panel Learn from panelists from companies like Uber, Google, Yahoo, YCombinator across roles like software engineering, product management, and data science about best practices on how to break into the industry, big company vs. small company, how Swarthmore helped their career trajectory and more.

From Good to Great: Lessons from the Tech Industry from Cathy Polinsky ‘99 A casual chat with Cathy Polinsky, former CTO @ Stitch Fix and former SVP of Engineering at Salesforce on her own take of From Good to Great. Hear her discuss what differentiates a great company, a great engineer, a great leader or a great mentor from just a good one as she shares life lessons and stories from her career journey in the tech industry.
