Launch Fellows

Launch Fellows is a mentorship program that matches distinguished underclassmen LaunchDeck members with an upperclassmen mentor with experience in their field of interest that will help guide the student as the explore what career they want to pursue and prepare to apply for internships. Some companies our mentors have worked at in the past include Facebook, Microsoft, Salesforce, Blackrock, and Bloomberg.

What tracks do we offer?

The Launch Fellows program offers various tracks spanning careers in technology and entrepreneurship for students to choose from. Fellows will be paired with mentors from their desired track. The tracks we currently offer are Software Engineering, Product Management, Data Science/Analytics, Venture Capital, Marketing, and Business Development/Sales.

Not sure which tracks you are interested in? Check out LaunchDeck's blog where we dive into the similarities and differences between these careers.

Eligibility

All freshman or sophomore (class of 2023 or 2024) members of LaunchDeck are eligible for this program. Don't worry if you don't have experience in your desired track! We are looking for your passion and dedication. If you are not already a member of LaunchDeck, join here.
